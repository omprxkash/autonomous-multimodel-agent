ReconStock

The only Shopify inventory sync that shows every change before it writes — and lets you undo it.

The problem

Multi-store Shopify merchants depend on sync apps that fail catastrophically: inventory zeroed or negated across thousands of SKUs, oversells, "$thousands in losses." Verified across three unrelated incumbent apps, most recently June 2026:

"…all inventory level for connected products in both stores is either zero or even negative." (2,000+ products)
Shopify App Store review, 2026-06-21 — re-fetched & verified

Every incumbent syncs first and apologizes later. None ships dry-run, diff preview, or rollback (live listings audit, 2026-07-11).

The product

A sync engine built around safety primitives: read-only dry-run by default, a diff preview of every write, an anomaly circuit-breaker ("this sync would zero 2,143 SKUs — halted"), and journaled one-click rollback. Working demo built; core flow verified by 34 automated end-to-end checks.

Why now / why us
