# Onboarding email sequence (drafts — nothing is sent anywhere)

Voice: foreman-calm. Short. Every email has one job.

---

## Email 0 — Waitlist confirmation (instant)
**Subject: You're on the list**

That's it — no daily drip, no "community." We'll email you once, when doors open, and
founding seats ($199 for life, 200 of them) go to this list first.

Meanwhile, two useful things, free:
- The Lapse Math sheet — print it, tape it in the van, know your own number.
- Our state rules table — what your state actually requires, with sources and a
  "verified/draft" flag on every row. If we've got your state wrong, reply and say so.
  We fix data fast; that's the whole product.

— DueCrew

## Email 1 — Day 0 after signup (account created)
**Subject: Ten minutes now, zero minutes for the rest of the year**

Fastest setup: photograph your license, your COIs, and your bond. Text them to the number
in the app. We type everything in and your board appears — usually within the hour.
That's not an AI gimmick; a human checks every date, because the whole point is that the
dates are right.

Rather do it yourself? Board → Add credential. Takes about 90 seconds each.

## Email 2 — Day 2 (if board has <2 credentials)
**Subject: Your board is still mostly empty**

Which usually means one of three things:
1. The photos are still in your camera roll — text them over, we'll do the rest.
2. You're not sure what counts. Rule of thumb: if a GC, a state board, or a city can stop
   you working over its date, it goes on the board. License, CE window, GL cert, workers'
   comp cert, auto, bond, local registrations.
3. This isn't for you — fair. One click at the bottom deletes the account and everything
   in it. No phone call, no "are you sure" gauntlet.

## Email 3 — Day 7
**Subject: What the amber card means**

Green: nothing to do. Amber: a date is inside 30 days — we've started nudging, and for
insurance we can copy your agent so the renewal starts without you chasing it.
Red: inside 14 days. The texts get louder, the renewal link and the fee are in them, and
the day before, we call.

One more thing worth doing this week: send your COI share link to the GC who asks most
often. It always serves the current certificate. You'll never email a PDF again.

## Email 4 — Day 30 (first month close)
**Subject: Your first month, in three numbers**

- Dates watched: {n}
- Nudges sent: {n}
- Days until your next expiration: {n}

If those numbers are boring, good. Boring is the product. The month it isn't boring is
the month it pays for the decade.

If anything on your board is wrong — a date, a rule, a fee — reply to this email. A human
reads it the same day.
