Straight answers

Is this another field-service suite?

No. No scheduling, no dispatch, no invoicing. DueCrew tracks the documents that let you work — licenses, CE, insurance, bonds — and nothing else. Small contractors keep telling the market the suites are “way too involved.” We agree.

Can't I just use my phone calendar?

You can, and most people do — right up until the year the reminder fires while you're on a roof, or the CE requirement changed, or the renewal needed hours you didn't have logged. A calendar knows a date. DueCrew knows the rule behind the date, watches the CE math, escalates across channels, and copies your insurance agent when it matters.

Which states do you cover?

The rules library launches with 10 states for electrical, plumbing, and HVAC, expanding weekly. Texas rules are verified against TDLR today; every row shows its verification status — we don't pretend coverage we don't have. Alerts on dates you enter work in any state from day one.

Is registry monitoring live?

Beta, Texas first. It checks the public state registry and confirms your status independently. Until your state is live, DueCrew works from the dates on your documents.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Export everything as a spreadsheet, one click, then we delete it. Cancellation is a button, not a phone call.
