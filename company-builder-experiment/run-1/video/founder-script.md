# ReconStock — Founder video script

~40–45 seconds. Calm single voiceover (Windows System.Speech, best available
en-US voice, rate slightly slow) over title cards + slow product footage.
Music bed ~20dB under the voice. Brand voice: calm, precise, zero hype.
Red-team rules: verified problem stated plainly; promise is "fails safe and
is fully reversible," never "never fails"; honest close; $29/mo named.

## Voiceover (read as one take, natural pauses at paragraph breaks)

In twenty twenty-five, a Shopify merchant watched a sync app set their entire
inventory to zero. Three hours of manual recovery. Thousands of dollars lost.
It keeps happening, across app after app, because sync tools write first and
ask never.

ReconStock is built the other way around. Every sync is previewed as a diff.
Every run can be a dry run. A circuit breaker halts anything destructive
before it writes. And one click rolls back anything you apply.

We don't promise a sync that never fails. Nobody honestly can. We promise a
sync that fails safe, and is fully reversible.

ReconStock. Safe inventory sync for multi-store Shopify merchants. From
twenty-nine dollars a month.
